First, I need to check the original responses. The answers included "la patate", "Patate", "Pomme de terre", and "Frites". Previously, these were grouped under "Patate / Pomme de terre / Frites". Now, I need to split them.

But wait, "la patate" and "Patate" are the same in French, both meaning potato. "Pomme de terre" is the formal term for potato. So those three should still be grouped together. However, "Frites" (fries) are a processed form, so they should be a separate category. That way, the user's concern about preservation is addressed.

So, the new normalized responses for the apocalypse question will have "Patate / Pomme de terre" and "Frites" as separate entries.
