C'est vraiment l'enfer leur com, serieux, y a pas un truc qui soit sur la meme page.....

You can earn your spot in the finals in 4 different ways, so let’s break them down together:

The first way is through WPN Qualifiers, WPN stores will host events in which the winner will be qualified to the finals.

The second way is through a Classic Qualifier during a Legacy Magic Showdown (a big festival-like event that will take place once each month starting from july 2022), Classic Qualifiers are competitive-level events with a capped number of players. The first 8 players will be qualified to the finals.

The third way is through a Grand Open Qualifier during a Legacy Magic Showdown (a big festival-like event that will take place once each month starting in July 2022), Grand Open Qualifiers are competitive-level events with an open number of players and the top 32 players in each Grand Open Qualifier will earn a spot in the finals.

The fourth way is through a Last Chance Qualifier. LCQs will take place the day before the Finals, will be competitive-level events with an open number of players, and will qualify the top 16 players to the finals.
