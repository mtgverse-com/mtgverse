It is not possible to directly install an Xbox wired controller on a Nintendo Switch. The Nintendo Switch uses its own proprietary controllers, such as the Joy-Con controllers and the Pro Controller, which are specifically designed to work with the Switch console.

However, if you still want to use an Xbox controller with your Nintendo Switch, you have a couple of options:

Wireless Adapter: You can use a third-party wireless adapter that allows you to connect your Xbox controller to the Nintendo Switch. These adapters typically connect to the Switch's USB port and emulate a Switch-compatible controller. Make sure to check the compatibility of the adapter with your specific Xbox controller model.

Wired Controller Adapter: Another option is to use a wired controller adapter. There are adapters available that allow you to connect an Xbox controller to the Switch's USB port. These adapters convert the Xbox controller's input into a format compatible with the Switch.

Third-Party Controllers: Alternatively, you can consider purchasing a third-party controller that is specifically designed to work with the Nintendo Switch. There are several options available on the market, including controllers that resemble the design of the Xbox controller.

Please note that using third-party adapters or controllers might not provide the exact same experience as using the original Switch controllers. It's always a good idea to research and read reviews before purchasing any third-party accessories to ensure compatibility and functionality.
